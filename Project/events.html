Junior Rangers

Every Thursday - Saturday (3 PM - 5 PM)


Looking for a fun way to spend your afternoon? Well look no further, bring your children down to Red Stone to experience a day with our park rangers. Here they will learn about what being a park ranger is all about as well as learn about our park's wildlife and geology.


Bird Watching

Every Wednesday (4 PM - 5 PM)


Join a park ranger expert and fellow bird watchers on a hike to sight some of our park's most fascinating birds. These birds include hawks, eagles, and falcons. Good luck!


Fauna Finding

Every Tuesday (4 PM - 5 PM)


Red Stone is home to many different types of Mojave Desert plants. On our Fauna Finding tour, a park ranger will guide you through a trail and identify these plants and their many uses. For example our most common Redstone flower, the Creosote was used by Native Americans for medicinal purposes.
